**Clairsentients** can sense the presence of other incarnated spirits, ghosts, or demons. Most incarnated spirits have this ability to varying degrees.

**Empaths** sense and experience the emotions of other incarnated spirits, ghosts, or demons.  Depending on the sensitivity of the empath, emotions may or may not need to be expressed through energy to be sensed.

**Aura Readers** see emotion, personality, or illness as a cloud of colors surrounding incarnated spirits, ghosts, or demons.

**Channelers** open themselves to possession, allowing ghosts or demons to communicate or act using their body.

**Mediums** with varying degrees of ability to see or hear other spiritual entities allow for communication between less sensitive incarnated spirits and ghosts or demons.

**Psychometric Psychics** attain partial access to [[Concepts/The Net\|The Net]]'s knowledge through touching objects and reading their histories.

**Claircognizants** attain partial access to The Net randomly or situationally. This knowledge may come to them through visions, dreams, or thoughts.
